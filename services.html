SERVICES

  • Receiving & Delivery

    – As product is received into our warehouse, we inspect all product to be able to provide a quick turnaround for replacement parts that may need to be ordered prior to installation in addition to reporting any possible concealed damage to our vendors.

  • Installation

    – We provide a full service of installation from a single office chair to a full building of office furniture. No job is too small. We carefully handle product and take all precautions to protect floors and doors. We are certified installers that provide you with meticulous installation, ensuring our product is level, flush and working to fit you and your needs properly before we leave.

  • Furniture Reconfiguration

    – Need to add more people in a tight space? Our professionals can work closely with you to reconfigure an existing space to expand capacity and usability for your work place.

  • Space Planning

    – Our design staff can space plan new and/or existing furniture for new and/or existing offices.

  • Corporate Relocation

    – We know that you are busy and sometimes you have to move. Don’t sweat it, let us take care of any corporate office relocation projects. We will work with you to either inventory existing furniture or provide new furniture to fit you with the best plan for your new space. We will coordinate the office move, work with IT, electricians and ensure you an easy relocation of your new space.

  • Manufacturer’s Warranty Work

    – We know not everything can stand the test of time. Luckily vendors carry warranty for their product. With access to over 100 commercial furniture lines, Bolt can repair and order new parts for all product that falls under manufacturer’s warranty.

  • Annual Maintenance & Touch‐Up Contracts

    – Not Ready to order new furniture but would like an office refresh? We can make your furniture look new again. We offer annual maintenance and touch-up contracts to carefully repair minor scratches and detail your commercial space.
